What is Proton?

Proton is a project started by Vexio, made to re-create Essentials, with Skript. Skript is a custom Minecraft coding Language created by Njolbrim. You can find more information here

What are the additions to Proton?

The additions, or addons, for Proton are Neutron & Electron. Both have there own separate features that Proton does not contain.

What is Neutron?

Neutron is the chat addon for Proton. Neutron is set to be an extremely simple & customizable chat plugin, integrated with Proton. Neutron will contain features such as changing a group's prefix, suffix, chat color, etc.

What is Electron?

Electron at the moment does currently have a solid use. Currently, I am trying to think of a use for it; because I really would like to use all atomic particles used in making up an atom.

How do I download & install Proton?

Installing The Proton Project on to your server is quite simple. To start, you will need to have installed Skript on your server. After so, restart your server. After restarting, download & extract proton.sk, and the other specified addons you chose, into the directory of plugins/Skript/scripts/. After that, restart your server!
